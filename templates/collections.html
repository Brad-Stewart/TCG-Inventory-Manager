How Collection Sharing Works
  • Create Template: When importing a CSV, check "Create Template" to make it shareable
  • Independent Copies: Each user gets their own copy - changes don't affect other users
  • Public vs Private: Private templates are only visible to you, public ones can be imported by anyone
  • Import Process: Importing creates new cards in your collection with default purchase prices of $0
  • Customization: After importing, you can edit quantities, conditions, purchase prices independently
